# POD Sync Button Guide

## Where to Find the Sync Button

### For Jetprint:
1. Go to: https://basketballgearstore.abacusai.app/admin/jetprint
2. Look at the TOP-RIGHT corner of the page
3. You'll see a button labeled "Sync Products" with a refresh icon

### For InterestPrint:
1. Go to: https://basketballgearstore.abacusai.app/admin/interestprint
2. Look at the TOP-RIGHT corner of the page
3. You'll see a button labeled "Sync Products" with a refresh icon

## What the Sync Button Does

When you click "Sync Products":
- Fetches new products from your POD provider (Jetprint or InterestPrint)
- Updates existing product information (prices, stock status)
- Adds any new products you created on their platform
- Shows a timestamp of when the last sync occurred

## How to Use It

1. Click the "Sync Products" button
2. Wait a few seconds (button will show "Syncing..." with spinning icon)
3. Success message will appear: "Products synced successfully!"
4. A green banner will show the last sync timestamp
5. Product list refreshes automatically
